About Donna

A writer for artists with real stories to tell.

Donna Block helps independent artists turn their background, creative process, and latest work into readable features that feel sincere instead of manufactured. The writing can support a release campaign, an EPK, social sharing, a press page, or a pitch to venues and collaborators.

The tone can flex from classic country warmth to modern Americana polish, with enough structure to make the artist's message easy to share.
