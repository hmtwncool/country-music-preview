About Donna

A feature writer for artists with real stories to tell.

Donna Block helps independent artists turn their background, creative process, and latest work into readable features that feel sincere instead of manufactured. Her work can support a release campaign, an EPK, social sharing, a press page, or a pitch to venues and collaborators.

The tone can flex from classic country warmth to modern Americana polish, while keeping the message direct enough for busy readers to understand and share.
